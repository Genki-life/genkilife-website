Why Genki is different

Quiet by design

No leaderboards. No guilt. A system that pays attention, so you can stop performing for your apps.

It notices, so you don't have to

Nova quietly reads your sleep, movement, and rhythm — and reflects what it sees. It observes; it never nags.

Grows at the speed of your life

Some weeks rise, some weeks rest. Genki softens when you need it to and moves when you're ready — no plan to fall behind on.

Your state matters more than your streak

There is nothing to keep alive, nothing to break. Genki tracks how you are — not how much you produced.

From the manifesto

Human beings are not productivity machines. We are living systems.

When body, mind, emotions, relationships, and creativity move together, we feel in flow. When they drift apart, we lose our rhythm. Genki is the feeling of being in rhythm with yourself — whether that rhythm is fast or slow, rising or resting.

Not maximizing output — but restoring rhythm. Not endless optimization — but sustainable aliveness.
